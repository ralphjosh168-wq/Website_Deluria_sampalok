Facts about Volcanoes

This video presents interesting facts about volcanoes.


Three types of volcanic activity are:

  • Active Volcanoes - These volcanoes are currently erupting or show signs of erupting in the near future.
  • Dormant Volcanoes - These volcanoes are not currently active but have the potential to erupt again.
  • Extinct Volcanoes - These volcanoes are not expected to erupt again as they have not erupted for a long time.
